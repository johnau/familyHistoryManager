Features for Family History Manager:
==========================================

1. Suggest link on typing family members or articles:
    When a user is typing into a Family Member Bio / Article / etc. the text should be monitored for Family Member
    names and Article names.
    eg. If a user typed "His father, Chris McSweeney", a suggestion should be made to convert
    "Chris McSweeney" to a link to the page of "Christopher Liguori McSweeney".
